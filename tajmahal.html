TAJ MAHAL

Taj Mahal
4 Days 3 Hotel nights
Uttar Pradesh : Agra 1N, Delhi : Delhi 2N. Visit: 2 Cities

Day 1-Delhi-Agra

Proceed to Agra. Visit Sikandra - the lasting place of Mughal Emperor Akbar, Agra Fort, Itimad-Ud-Daulah and Taj Mahal - One of the Seven Wonder of the World.

Day 2-Agra-Delhi

Proceed to Delhi. On arrival visit Madame Tussauds Museum known for life-size wax replicas of celebrities & historic icons in themed galleries. Visit Lotus Temple, Akshardham temple, Light & sound show of Akshardham temple.

Day 3-Shimla-Kufri-Shimla

Visit Kufri Fun Campus (Guest can enjoy various rides & activities at their own cost). Later visit Kufri - Winter Sports Capital, Indira Bungalow and Himalayan Zoo (subject to operations). In the evening visit the famous Mall road, Scandal Point and Shimla Church.
Extra Topping : Pastry / Softy & Tea/Coffee at National bakery on Mall Road. Tea/Coffee @ Kufri.

Day 4-Delhi-Kingdom of Dreams

Arrive at Delhi Airport and report to the coach. Visit the Red Fort. The fort has Diwan-i-Am, Diwan-i-Khas and the Pearl Mosque within itself. Proceed to Kingdom of Dreams which brings to you a blend of India's culture, heritage, art, crafts, cuisine and performing arts buttressed with the mind boggling technological wizardry of today. Enjoy dinner and a breathtaking show at Kingdom of Dreams.
