OOTY

S0 - MUNNAR KODAIKANAL OOTY
7 Days 6 Hotel Nights
Kerala : Munnar 2N, Tamil Nadu : Kodaikanal 2N, Ooty 2N Visit:

Day 1-Kochin-Munnar

Arrive at Cochin, report to coach and proceed Munnar. Arrive in Munnar in the evening.
Extra Topping : Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink en-route to Munnar.

Day 2-Munnar

Enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam. View Tea Gardens, visit Echo Point and Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park.
Extra Topping : Pineapple slice and Tea/ Coffee at Echo Point, Munnar

Day 3-Munnar-Kodaikanal

Proceed to Kodaikanal. On arrival visit the Byrant Park and enjoy boat ride in Kodai lake. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening.
Extra Topping : Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink en-route to Kodaikanal.

Day 4-Kodaikanal

Visit the Kurinji Temple, Coaker's Walk, Silver Cascade Fall, Pillar Rocks Moier Point and Green Valley view point. Free time for shopping in the evening.
Extra Topping : Ice-cream during Kodaikanal city tour.

Day 5-Kodaikanal-Ooty

Proceed to Ooty. Arrive at Ooty in the evening.
Extra Topping : Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink en-route to Ooty.

Day 6-Ooty

Enjoy the city tour of Ooty that includes Government Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta Peak, Enjoy boat ride in Ooty Lake. Free time for shopping in the evening.
Extra Topping : Frooti during Backwater Ride and Coconut water at Kumarkom Bird Sanctuary.

Day 7-Ooty-Departure from coimbatore

Board the Train / flight for your hometown. Tour Concludes.
