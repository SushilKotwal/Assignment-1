SHIMLA

HC-SHIMLA MANALI
7 Days 6 Hotel Nights
Punjab: Chandigarh 2N, Pinjore. Himachal Pradesh : Shimla 2N, Kufri, Kullu, Manali 4N, Rohtang Snow Point, Solang.
Visit: 8 Cities

Day 1-Chandigarh

Arrive at Chandigarh Airport, report to coach and proceed to hotel.
Extra Topping : Tea/coffee at Chandigarh.

Day 2-Chandigarh-Shimla

Visit Sukhana Lake, Rose Garden, Rock Garden. Later proceed to Shimla. En-route visit Pinjore Garden. Arrive in Shimla in the evening.
Extra Topping : Frooti at Pinjore Garden and Tea/Coffee En-route Chandigarh - Shimla.

Day 3-Shimla-Kufri-Shimla

Visit Kufri Fun Campus (Guest can enjoy various rides & activities at their own cost). Later visit Kufri - Winter Sports Capital, Indira Bungalow and Himalayan Zoo (subject to operations). In the evening visit the famous Mall road, Scandal Point and Shimla Church.
Extra Topping : Pastry / Softy & Tea/Coffee at National bakery on Mall Road. Tea/Coffee @ Kufri.

Day 4-Shimla-Manali

Proceed to Manali. En-route pass by Pandoh Dam, Beas river, Sutlaj river, Beas Sutlaj Link Project, Kullu Valley. Visit Shawl Factory at Kullu. Arrive at Manali in the evening.
Extra Topping : Lays, Tea / Coffee / Soft Drink en-route from Shimla to Manali. Tea/Coffee at Kullu Shawl Factory .

Day 5-Manali-Solang-Manali

In the morning explore the ancient temples like Jagatsukh Gayatri Mandir, Vashista Kund, Hidimba Mandir . Then proceed to Solang Valley - A splendid valley between Solang village and Beas Kund which offers the view of glaciers and snow capped mountains and peaks which has fine ski slopes. Enjoy rope way ride.
Extra Topping : Tea/Coffee @ Solang Valley.

Day 6-Manali-Rohtang Snow point-Manali

Enjoy spectacular drive towards Rohtang snow point. Free time to play and enjoy in the snow. In the evening enjoy Himachali Folk dance.
Extra Topping : Coffee at Snow Point, Rohtang. Tea/Coffee at Mandi.
Notes : Rohtang pass is 51 kms. away from Manali. This Pass is not accessible for almost 8 months in a year as it is covered by snow. We generally visit the snow point on Rohtang road, which has been permitted by the Border Security Force.

Day 7-Departure from Chandigarh

Proceed to Chandigarh airport Board the flight for your home town. Tour Concludes.
